I am a Schmidt Science Fellow at the University of Toronto. Previously, I was a Rhodes Scholar at the University of Oxford where I completed my PhD in Condensed Matter Physics.

I unite experiments, computation, and machine learning (ML) to develop materials for clean energy, particularly photovoltaics. My research is outward-facing, engaging with the public, industry, and policymakers.

Want to collaborate? Hear about my science? Chat about a problem you have? Get in touch: suhas.mahesh@utoronto.ca.

I moonlight as an expert of classical Sanskrit and Prakrit. Check out my book: How to Love in Sanskrit (HarperCollins 2024)2. Under contract: Nilakantha’s Victory for HUP’s Murty Classical Library. I’m also a co-creator of ambuda.org
